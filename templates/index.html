Welcome to the MeitY Cloud Service Provider Audit Portal

This portal facilitates the empanelment and audit process for Cloud Service Providers (CSPs) in India, ensuring security, compliance, and transparency. It provides a streamlined workflow for CSPs to submit requests and for MeitY Reviewers, STQC Auditors, and Scientist F authorities to manage and track the audit lifecycle.
